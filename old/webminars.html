Online seminars and talks in Logic

Due to the current situation in the world, some Logic seminars will be held online for some time. The following is a list of some online seminars, a list of recorded ordered by spaker can be found here or at the bottom in the Weminar link.
I will try to keep the list up to date. If you know about a seminar that will be held online and the organizers want to share the information about it, let me know; information about seminars that upload the talks is also welcome. Other webpages about online seminars in mathematics:
  • Logic talks online [Webpage]
  • Math Seminars [Webpage]
The information about online seminars and events is divided in three sections. A schedule by day with the usual time (local time and GMT) of the seminars, the seminars with a webpage have a link on the information. An upcoming section with information about the next seminar talks (speaker, title, etc). In the last section you can find videos of the talks that were recorded, for those that feel more confortable watching the video than attending the online seminar. Only public information is shared in the list (seminar's webpage and the information that organizers want to make public), you can find the link to the talks in the respective webpages or by contacting the organizers.

Note: Double check the local time at seminar's webpage when planning to attend to the seminars to not miss them.
